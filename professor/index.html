Research Interests

  • Development materials showing super-low friction coefficient
  • Development and Wear performance evaluation of high entropy alloys
  • Candidate material study through by coating and heat treating with conventional metals considering economics
  • Analysis and mitigation of wear occurred by biomass preprocessing and pretreatment
  • Bench-scale 2-body & 3-body abrasion, and errosion wear tester design, development, and performance evaluation
  • Electrochemical analysis and corrosion evaluation of metals
  • Analysis of the tribological performance of materials and wear evaluation (microstructure & mechanical
    properties)
  • The design & fabrication of super-hard materials with high wear resistance, favorable fracture toughness, and low friction coefficient
  • Plasmonic properties of coated nano-structure
  • Nanoplasmonics-Surface Enhanced Raman Scattering (SERS)
  • Analysis of nanostructures-mechanical characterization & wetting characteristics
  • The design and fabrication of nanostructured materials (Anodic Aluminum Oxide)
